About Me

I've been writing code since I was 11 — self-taught from the start, driven by the same instinct I still follow today: if something can be built, build it yourself and make it work better than what existed before.

That instinct scaled into a career. I specialize in systems that do the work for you — eliminating operational friction through intelligent automation, agentic AI workflows, and a rock-solid data foundation. My focus is enterprise architecture: designing resilient, integrated systems that automate critical supply chain and financial workflows end-to-end.

In practice that means building production agentic pipelines and recommendation engines that leverage AI for contextual reasoning while keeping deterministic operations reliable and predictable. It means owning financial data integrity — diagnosing high-risk inconsistencies and holding strict accountability for the numbers. And it means leading technical teams to enforce the architectural standards that let a system grow without becoming a liability.
